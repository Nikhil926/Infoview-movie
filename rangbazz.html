ABOUT THE WEB SERIES

"RANGBAAZ"

Rangbaaz, is a ZEE5 original web series
released on 22 December 2018. It is a crime
thriller series that is set against the rustic
background of the Gorakhpur of the 1990's.

Rangbaaz is based on a true story of
Shri Prakash Shukla, who was a notorious
and most wanted criminal/gangster of Gorakhpur,
Uttar Pradesh. This series shows his journey from
a DDU student to becoming the second most wanted
criminal in India. Other characters related to
Shree Prakash's life include Ram Shankar Tiwari
and Chandra Bhan Singh inspired by Suraj Bhan Singh,

It is a gangster series but not like we have
seen earlier.. actors are amazing.. direction is
also too good but scenes are intermixed at times..
story is well built and moreover it is inspired by
true incidents..you should definitely watch it
