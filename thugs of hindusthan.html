Review of THUGS OF HINDUSTAN

Thugs of Hindostan is a 2018 Bollywood action-adventure,
written and directed by Vijay Krishna Acharya.The movie stars
Amitabh Bachchan, Aamir Khan, Katrina Kaif,Fatima Sana Shaikh, etc., in lead roles.

The movie is based on Philip Meadows Taylor's 1839 novel
Confessions of a Thug, which tells a fictional story
set between 1790 and 1805. The film depicts the story of a
group of Thugs who were led by Khudabaksh Azaad to free
Hindustan from the rule of the British East India Company.

Thugs of Hindostan is produced by Aditya Chopra under the
banner Yash Raj Films. The movie is made with a whooping
budget of ?300 crore. The movie has its music composed by
Ajay Gogavale and Atul Gogavale.
